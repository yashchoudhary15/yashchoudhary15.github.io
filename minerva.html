Minerva Capital Group

Website redesign

Overview

Minerva is a for-purpose investment fund company focusing on social impact investment. It has its global network spread across over 40 countries. My role was to make a responsive redesign the existing website of Minerva, in order to increase the traffic on it and attract the target audience.

Client

Minerva Capital Group: Miami, Florida, USA

My role

Web designer

Design tools

Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop, Pen and Paper

Client brief

Since there was no chance to meet the client over a coffee because of the distances, I had zoom meetings with them to understand their requirements and their target audience.

They wanted the website design to be very minimal, yet attractive, which could reflect a futuristic touch. The target audience included early stage companies working for creating a social impact through innovative technologies, and investors who would invest in them.

I discussed about the information architecture and different sections to add in the website, and proceeded to start designing.

Wireframes

Sketches

I began with listing down all the sections to be designed and drawing low fidelity wireframes, i.e. sketches to iterate quickly and get feedback.

High fidelity wireframes

Next, I made several variations of a single page website having different visual themes.

Final design

Conclusions

I shared the prototype link for generated from Adobe XD to the developers for development. The redesigned website showed an increase in conversion rate by around 60%, where the key performance indicator (KPI) was 'contacting Minerva through the contact us section'.
